PROFILE: NNAEMEKA

I don't just write code.
I design outcomes.

I am a Business Automation Architect based in Lagos, Nigeria. I specialize in replacing chaotic human workflows with self-healing, intelligent code.

The Evolution

I started as a web designer, building beautiful interfaces. But I quickly realized that a pretty website doesn't solve operational chaos. Clients were getting leads, but they were losing them in messy spreadsheets and forgotten emails.

I pivoted from "designing pages" to "architecting systems." Today, I use tools like n8n, Python, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to build invisible machines that run businesses in the background.

My Core Belief

"The system is the business. If your revenue depends on you remembering to send an email, you don't have a business; you have a job. Automation buys back your freedom."

Operating Principles

01. Reliability First

A flashy automation that breaks once a week is worthless. I build robust error handling and self-healing logic into every workflow.

02. Data Privacy

Your data is your asset. I prioritize secure authentication, local AI models where necessary, and strict access controls.

03. Documentation

I don't gatekeep my work. Every system I build comes with clear documentation so your team owns the process, not me.
