AI Engineering Methodology

01

Prompt Engineering & Testing

AI is only as good as its instructions. I spend hours refining the "System Prompt" to ensure the AI behaves consistently, adheres to your brand voice, and never hallucinates facts.

02

Privacy-First Architecture

For sensitive industries (Legal, Health), we don't send data to OpenAI. I deploy Local LLMs (Llama 3, Mistral) using tools like Ollama. Your data stays on your server.

03

Human-in-the-Loop Safeguards

We never let AI autopilot critical decisions initially. I build "Approval Nodes" where a human reviews the AI's output (Draft Email, Score) before it is sent.
