01. Automated Lead Management System for Landscaping Businesses.

A "Digital Dispatcher" that intercepts traffic from Google Ads, analyzes the customer's written intent, and instantly routes them to either Emergency Dispatch or Sales.

Target: Landscaping & Tree Services

THE PAIN POINT

Emergency leads (fallen trees) get buried in the same inbox as "Can I get a quote for next month?" This delay causes high-value emergency jobs to go to competitors who answer faster.

THE LOGIC FIX

We intercept the lead at the Google Ad click. The system reads the inquiry text for keywords like "Danger", "Tree", or "Storm" and triggers an SMS alert to the field crew within 2 seconds.
