01. The Digital Foreman

A "Digital Dispatcher" that intercepts traffic from Google Ads, analyzes the customer's written intent, and instantly routes them to either Emergency Dispatch or Sales.

Target: Landscaping & Tree Services

THE PAIN POINT

Emergency leads (fallen trees) get buried in the same inbox as "Can I get a quote for next month?" This delay causes high-value emergency jobs to go to competitors who answer faster.

THE LOGIC FIX

We intercept the lead at the Google Ad click. The system reads the inquiry text for keywords like "Danger", "Tree", or "Storm" and triggers an SMS alert to the field crew within 2 seconds.

02. The Algorithmic Gatekeeper

A logic-based application engine that audits prospects, calculates a "Fit Score" in real-time, and only permits qualified leads to book a call.

Target: Coaches & Consultants

THE PAIN POINT

"Calendar Congestion." You get 50 leads a month, but 40 are unable to afford your retainer. You waste 20+ hours on calls that never close.

THE LOGIC FIX

Instead of a contact form, we use a Quiz. The system scores answers in the background. High scores (>70) get an email with a booking link. Low scores get a helpful downsell course.
